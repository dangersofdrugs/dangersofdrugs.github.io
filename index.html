What is our goal?

As you can see in our title, our goal is spread awareness of the dangers of drugs. Not mainly the drugs used as medicine, the ones that are use to get "high" or go insane. Drugs like crack, ecstasy, and heroin.

Dangerous Drugs

Information on the effects of dangerous drugs and statistics on the effects in the past

  • Cocaine

    Cocaine is a drug used to affect your mental state, and send you into a different reality. It also gives people a intense feeling of happiness, or agitation.

  • Cannabis

    Cannabis is a drug grown from a Indian hemp plants. The drug is able to make you "high", "high" meaning it potentially slows down the activity of the nervous system and messages from the brain to the body.

  • Ecstasy

    The actual name for it is MDMA (3,4-methylenedioxy-methamphetamine) it is a psychoactive drug that is mostly used as a recreational one. The after effects can change sensations, have increased energy, empathy, and have heightened pleasure.
